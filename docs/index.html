Hi, I'm Miranda

我是陳虹佑，畢業於銘傳大學商品設計學系，有七年的工業包裝設計經驗。在前份工作中，接觸到電子看板的介面設計，開始了解UI介面設計的相關資訊。很開心有機會參加中興大學大數據系統開發-全端工程師養成班的訓練課程，學習了前端與後端的技術，擁有程式基礎，對於介面設計將有所幫助。執行團體專題過程中，主要負責企劃與設計，完成Wireframe, Mockup設計稿後，接著進行前端切版與後端資料處理。透過專案的開發流程，學習到如何以較為全觀的視角去完善網站以及滿足使用者需求。

My name is Miranda. I graduated from the Department of Product Design of Ming Chuan University. I have seven years’ experience as a packaging engineer. I got to know about interface design in previous job. I think it’s a very promising industry. I’m glad that I participated in the full-end engineer training course of National Chung Hsing University. I have learned the frontend and backend technologies for web development, and which will be helpful for interface design. I was in charge of plan and design in group project. After finishing the wireframe and mockup, I proceeded to do web layout and backend development. During the project process, I learned how to meet user needs and develop a website by the big picture. It was a valuable experience.
